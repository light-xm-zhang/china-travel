🏯 Beijing 北京

Sep–Oct & Apr–May | 3–5 days

Attractions

Forbidden City

World's largest imperial palace, 980 buildings. Book 7–10 days ahead. Closed Mondays. ¥60.

Great Wall – Mutianyu

Best section for first-timers. Cable car + toboggan (~¥140). Leave by 7 AM. ¥40–45.

Temple of Heaven

Ming masterpiece. Visit early morning for locals doing tai chi in the park.

Summer Palace

Imperial garden with Kunming Lake. The Long Corridor is the world's longest painted corridor.

Hutongs

Wander Nanluoguxiang and Drum Tower alleys for authentic old Beijing life.

🌃 Shanghai 上海

Mar–May & Oct–Nov | 2–4 days

Attractions

The Bund

Iconic waterfront with colonial buildings and Pudong skyline. Best at dusk before 10 PM lights-out.

French Concession

Plane trees, lane houses, boutique cafes on Wukang and Anfu Roads. Best by bicycle.

Yu Garden

Ming dynasty garden + bazaar. Nanxiang xiaolongbao nearby. Beautifully lit at night.

Nanjing Road

Premier shopping street, 5.5 km from the Bund to People's Square.

Shanghai Tower

632m, world's 2nd tallest. 118th floor observation deck, ~¥180. Go on a clear day.

🗿 Xi'an 西安

Mar–May & Sep–Nov | 3–4 days

Attractions

Terracotta Warriors

8,000 life-sized warriors. Book 7 days ahead. ¥120. Arrive by 9 AM. Allow 3+ hours.

Ancient City Wall

Best-preserved in China (14 km). Bike at sunset from Yongning Gate – about 2 hours.

Muslim Quarter

Not just one street – head to Sajinqiao where locals eat. Great Mosque is a Tang Dynasty gem.

Big Wild Goose Pagoda

Tang Dynasty (652 AD). Nearby Joy City 4F viewing platform for the perfect photo.

Shaanxi History Museum

World-class Tang Dynasty collection. Free but book 7 days ahead (tickets at 10:00 & 18:00).

🐼 Chengdu 成都

Apr & mid-Oct | 3–4 days

Attractions

Giant Panda Base

Arrive by 7:15 AM – pandas most active 8–10 AM. By 11 AM most are napping. ¥55–60.

Jinli Ancient Street

Lantern-lit street next to Wuhou Shrine. Best at dusk. Try Sichuan peppercorn ice cream.

People's Park & Heming Teahouse

Jasmine tea in bamboo chairs, mahjong games, ear-cleaning. Pure Chengdu slow life.

Wenshu Monastery

Tang Dynasty temple – city's most peaceful spot. Free. Excellent vegetarian restaurant.

Leshan Giant Buddha

71m Buddha carved into a cliff (713 AD). ~2h from Chengdu. Boat for best frontal view.

⛰️ Guilin 桂林

Apr–May & Sep–Oct | 3–5 days

Attractions

Li River Bamboo Rafting

Float past 20 RMB note scenery. Xingping section ~¥90–150. Best 7–8 AM for misty views.

Yangshuo Countryside

Rent e-bike (~¥30/day), cycle the Ten-Mile Gallery through karst peaks and rice paddies.

Xianggong Mountain

Best panoramic view over Li River's first bend. 15-min climb. Arrive before dawn.

Longji Rice Terraces

650-year-old Dragon's Backbone terraces. Best May–Oct. ~2.5h from Guilin.

Xingping Ancient Town

1,700-year-old town. Stand at the 20 RMB note photo spot.

🏝️ Xiamen 厦门

Oct–Apr | 2–3 days

Attractions

Gulangyu Island

Car-free UNESCO island of colonial villas. Ferry ¥35 round trip. Book 7 days ahead. 7:30 AM ferry for empty streets.

Sunlight Rock

Highest point (92.7m), 360° views. Combo ticket 5 attractions: ¥90.

Shuzhuang Garden & Piano Museum

1913 seaside garden. 100+ antique pianos inside. Gulangyu = Piano Island.

Zhongshan Road & Eighth Market

Arcade-lined pedestrian street. Eighth Market nearby for fresh seafood and street eats.

Nanputuo Temple

1,000-year-old temple. Free. Climb the trail behind for coastline views.

🌊 Hangzhou 杭州

Mar–May & Sep–Oct | 2–3 days

Attractions

West Lake

UNESCO World Heritage. Bike the 15 km loop. Su Causeway at sunrise. Free.

Lingyin Temple

1,700-year-old Buddhist temple. Feilai Feng grottoes: 300+ stone carvings. ~¥75.

Longjing Tea Plantations

Visit during spring harvest (Mar–Apr). Walk Longjing Village to Nine Creeks.

Xixi Wetland Park

Traditional boats in reed waterways. Beautiful when persimmons turn orange in autumn. ~¥80.

🏔️ Zhangjiajie 张家界

Apr–May & Sep–Oct | 3–4 days

Attractions

National Forest Park

3,000+ pillars, Yuanjiajie (Avatar) area is the highlight. ¥228 (valid 4 days). Allow 2 full days.

Tianmen Mountain

World's longest cable car (7.5 km). Glass skywalk on vertical cliffs. ¥258. Book ahead.

Grand Canyon Glass Bridge

World's highest glass-bottom bridge (430m long, 300m high). ¥128.

Bailong Elevator

World's tallest outdoor elevator (326m) in a cliff face. 88-second ascent. ¥72.

🥟 Guangzhou 广州

Oct–Apr | 2–3 days

Attractions

Canton Tower

600m iconic tower. 488m observation deck with glass floor. Night light show. ~¥150.

Chen Clan Academy

Stunning 1894 complex with intricate ceramic, wood, and stone carvings. ~¥10.

Shamian Island

Tree-lined former European concession. Colonial architecture, cafes, quiet streets.

Pearl River Night Cruise

See Canton Tower, bridges, and skyline illuminated. ~¥60–120 depending on boat.

Yuexiu Park

Guangzhou's largest park. The Five Rams Statue is the city's symbol. Free.

🚀 Shenzhen 深圳

Oct–Apr | 2 days

Attractions

Window of the World

108 replicas of world landmarks in one park. Eiffel Tower, Pyramids, Taj Mahal. ~¥220.

Lianhuashan Park

Hilltop park with panoramic city views and a giant Deng Xiaoping bronze statue.

Dafen Oil Painting Village

World's largest oil painting production center. Watch artists at work, buy originals.

OCT Loft Creative Park

Converted factory turned hip arts district. Galleries, cafes, design shops.

Shenzhen Bay Park

9 km waterfront park with Hong Kong skyline views. Best at sunset.

🌆 Chongqing 重庆

Mar–May & Oct–Nov | 2–3 days

Attractions

Hongyadong

11-story stilted building complex on a cliff. Stunning when lit at night. Free to view.

Ciqikou Old Town

1,000-year-old porcelain port town. Narrow lanes, tea houses, street food.

Yangtze River Cableway

Iconic cable car across the Yangtze. 4-minute ride, ¥20 one-way. A moving viewing platform.

Dazu Rock Carvings

UNESCO World Heritage – 50,000 Buddhist statues from 9th–13th century. ~2h from city.

Liziba Light Rail Station

The famous station where trains pass through a residential building. Pure Chongqing.

🏛️ Nanjing 南京

Mar–May & Oct–Nov | 2–3 days

Attractions

Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum

Grand memorial at the top of 392 steps on Purple Mountain. Free (book ahead).

Ming City Wall

25 km of 600-year-old walls – among the longest city walls in the world.

Confucius Temple (Fuzimiao)

Riverside temple complex with traditional architecture, shops, and night boat rides.

Nanjing Massacre Memorial

Solemn museum documenting WWII history. Deeply moving, architecturally striking. Free.

Xuanwu Lake

Large urban lake with five islands, willow-lined paths, and city skyline views.
